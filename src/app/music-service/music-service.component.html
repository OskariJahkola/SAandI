Purity Music

From mellow tunes to funcky riffs, we play them all!

Our talented musicians provide excellent quality music for any event! From birthday parties, weddings, graduations to playing for senior citizens, we fit them all! We believe that music connects us in the deepest way possible, just like any great therapy. And as we are too many count, we have different reactions to different genres, hence our service of providing any genre to your fitting. Please join us, in the magic world of music and transend into a full relaxation and enjoy your moment!

Purity Music has founded by a small local band in Lappeenranta, who believed in the power of music. Wanting to cover all genres and in that way be able to provide to everyones need, the small company quickly expanded to acquire talents from all corners of the nation, currently empolying 50 talented individuals.

Also available on all major holidays!
