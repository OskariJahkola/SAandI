Recycle4Me has you covered!


Recycle4Me is a service that allows you to request a pickup service for your waste that the general garbage service doesn’t collect, i.e. large furniture, old appliances, and other problem waste.

The service is provided free of charge because the cost of operation is insignificant compared to the ecological value gained by providing a way to reduce unsorted waste.

If you have waste that needs special handling, request a pickup and we’ll handle it from there! If you can’t be around personally to hand out said waste, leave it at an appointed spot for pickup.


How to use Recycle4me?

1. Authenticate yourself with the service, we provide easy acces with social media logins (Google and Facebook) 2. Fill your pick-up information in "Request pick-up" - tab 3. Make a request 4. Enjoy!
